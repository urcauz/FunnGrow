Frequently Asked Questions

How old do you need to be to join Funngro as a teen freelancer?

Teens aged 13 to 19 can create profiles on Funngro. For users under 18, we require parental consent during registration. Our platform is designed specifically for student freelancers who want to gain real work experience while still in school.

Is Funngro safe for teenagers?

Yes, safety is our top priority. All companies are verified before they can post projects. We provide secure payment processing, allow parental oversight, and have a dedicated support team monitoring all interactions. Teens can work in a protected environment while building professional experience.

What types of projects can teens work on?

Teen freelancers on Funngro work on diverse projects including graphic design, content writing, social media management, video editing, web development, data entry, research, digital marketing, and more. We match teens with projects that fit their skills and interests.

How do payments work for teen freelancers?

Teens set their own rates or apply for projects with fixed budgets. Once work is completed and approved, payments are processed securely through our platform. Funds can be transferred to bank accounts or digital wallets with parental approval for minors.

How much does it cost to hire teen interns through Funngro?

Companies pay project-based rates negotiated directly with teen freelancers, plus a small platform fee. Rates are typically 30-50% lower than professional freelancers, making it affordable for startups and small businesses while ensuring teens earn fair compensation.

Are teen freelancers on Funngro qualified?

Every teen creates a detailed profile showcasing their skills, portfolio, and education. Many have completed online courses, built personal projects, or have prior experience. Companies can review portfolios, read reviews from previous clients, and conduct interviews before hiring.

Can companies hire teens for long-term internships?

Absolutely. Funngro supports both one-time projects and ongoing teen internships. Many companies start with a trial project and transition successful teens into long-term part-time roles. This flexibility works well for student schedules and company needs.

What makes Funngro different from other freelance platforms?

Funngro is purpose-built for connecting student freelancers with companies. Unlike general platforms, we focus exclusively on teen talent, provide age-appropriate safety features, offer parental controls, and create opportunities specifically designed for young professionals building their first portfolios.
